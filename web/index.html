The Roman Empire was the Mediterranean super power from 27 BCE to 476 AD after the fall of the Roman Republic.

The Roman Empire is famous for its military legions, impressive infrastructure, and lasting cultural influence on Western civilization.

The empire stretched across three continents and was one of the most powerful civilizations in history.
